Keyboard

  • [W][A][S][D] and Arrow Keys to move
  • [Enter] to enter or exit a bulding or a vehicle
  • [Space] to lay down, or take of with a helicopter
  • [Alt] to strafe
  • [Ctrl] to shoot
  • [N] to toggle night vision goggles
  • [F] to go fullscreen
  • [F8] to connect to Heat Zeeker Control

Android Heat Zeeker Control

[...] If you have an android device you can install Heat Zeeker Control from Play Store. Then you can connect by pressing [F8] your game to your device.

Shop

[...] In the game there are shops to buy in game consumable items. Find the shop. Buy the items.

! First we will log you in to FACEBOOK and then to PAYPAL, takes a few...

Once logged in to FACEBOOK the game will check, if you already have bought a shotgun. If not, it will try to open a popup.

If popup is blocked, it needs to be manually opened.

To complete the purchase, you need to log into PAYPAL.

Once the purchase is complete, the next time you click on the shotgun in the shop, the game realizes you already bought it and skips the payment.
